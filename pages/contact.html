Frequently Asked Questions

How long do commissions take?

Commission timelines vary by complexity and size. Portrait commissions typically take 4-6 weeks, while larger abstract pieces may require 8-12 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, I ship worldwide. All artworks are professionally packaged and insured. Shipping costs vary by destination and artwork size. International buyers are responsible for any customs duties.

Can I visit your studio?

Studio visits are welcome by appointment. I love sharing my creative process and discussing artwork in person. Please contact me to schedule a visit during regular studio hours.

What are your commission rates?

Commission rates depend on size, medium, and complexity. I provide detailed quotes after our initial consultation. A 50% deposit is required to begin work, with the balance due upon completion.
