About Elizabeth

Artist, photographer, and digital art pioneer exploring the delicate balance between light and shadow, emotion and form.

Artist Statement

Elizabeth Thatchers is a contemporary artist whose work explores the delicate balance between light and shadow, emotion and form. With over two decades of experience, her artistic journey spans multiple mediums including photography, digital art, and traditional painting techniques.

Born and raised in the Pacific Northwest, Elizabeth's deep connection to nature profoundly influences her artistic vision. Her landscapes capture the ethereal beauty of misty coastlines and ancient forests, while her portrait work reveals the inner essence of her subjects.

Her abstract compositions push the boundaries of traditional art, incorporating modern digital techniques with classical principles of composition and color theory. Elizabeth's work has been exhibited in galleries across the United States and internationally.

Currently based in her studio overlooking the coast, Elizabeth continues to create works that invite viewers into contemplative spaces where art becomes a bridge between the external world and inner experience.

Education & Background

Elizabeth holds a Master of Fine Arts from the Pacific Northwest College of Art, where she specialized in mixed media and contemporary photography. Her academic focus on the intersection of traditional and digital mediums continues to influence her work today.

Artistic Philosophy

"Art is not just about creating beauty—it's about capturing moments of truth that resonate beyond the surface. Each piece I create is an invitation to pause, to feel, and to see the world through a different lens."
